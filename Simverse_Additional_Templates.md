## Additional Race Templates

### Halfling (20 CP)
**Attribute Modifiers:**
- DX +2 (20 CP)
- ST -2 (-10 CP)
- CH +1 (10 CP)

**Included Perks:**
- Lucky Escape (10 CP) - Once per session, reroll any one failed roll
- Brave (+2 vs Fear effects) - Built into template

**Racial Traits:**
- **Small Size**: Half normal height, Speed 3.5 instead of 5
- **Sneaky**: +2 to Stealth checks
- **Resistant**: +2 to resist poison and disease
- **Keen Senses**: +1 to Perception
- **Lifespan**: ~150 years

**Starting Equipment:** None (from profession)

---

### Orc (15 CP)
**Attribute Modifiers:**
- ST +3 (15 CP)
- EN +2 (10 CP)
- IN -1 (-10 CP)
- CH -1 (-10 CP)

**Included Perks:**
- Tough (10 CP)
- High Pain Threshold (10 CP)

**Racial Traits:**
- **Powerful Build**: Carry capacity ×1.5
- **Aggressive**: +2 to Intimidation
- **Darkvision**: See in darkness up to 20m
- **Relentless**: When reduced to 0 Vitality, can take one more action before unconscious
- **Lifespan**: ~60 years

**Starting Equipment:** None (from profession)

---

### Half-Elf (15 CP)
**Attribute Modifiers:**
- DX +1 (10 CP)
- AW +1 (5 CP)
- CH +1 (10 CP)
- EN -1 (-5 CP)
- ST -1 (-5 CP)

**Included Perks:**
- Versatile Learner (10 CP) - Choose two skills to learn at half cost

**Racial Traits:**
- **Mixed Heritage**: Benefit from both human and elf gear/magic
- **Low-Light Vision**: No penalty in dim light (not full darkness)
- **Diplomatic**: +1 to social skills
- **Adaptable**: +1 to learning new languages
- **Lifespan**: ~180 years

**Starting Equipment:** None (from profession)

---

### Dragonborn (40 CP)
**Attribute Modifiers:**
- ST +2 (10 CP)
- EN +1 (5 CP)
- CH +1 (10 CP)

**Included Perks:**
- Draconic Breath (15 CP) - See below
- Tough (10 CP)
- Scales +2 AR (10 CP) - Natural armor

**Racial Traits:**
- **Draconic Breath**: Once per short rest, exhale destructive energy in 4m cone. Choose damage type at creation (fire, cold, lightning, acid, poison). Targets dodge (DX/2) or take 2d6 damage.
- **Natural Armor**: +2 AR from scales, stacks with worn armor
- **Imposing**: +2 to Intimidation
- **Draconic Ancestry**: Choose color, determines breath type and resistance
- **Lifespan**: ~80 years

**Starting Equipment:** None (from profession)

- IN +3 (30 CP)

**Included Perks:**

---

### Tiefling (30 CP)
**Attribute Modifiers:**
- CH +2 (20 CP)
- IN +1 (10 CP)
- WS +1 (10 CP)

**Included Perks:**
- Infernal Heritage (20 CP) - See below
- Darkvision (included in heritage)

**Racial Traits:**
- **Infernal Heritage**: Can cast Darkness (3m radius, 10 minutes) once per long rest. Resistance to fire damage (half damage from fire).

- **Hellish Appearance**: -2 to reactions with common folk, +2 to Intimidation
- **Infernal Legacy**: +2 to Occult Knowledge
- **Lifespan**: ~100 years

**Starting Equipment:** None (from profession)

## Mixing Multiple Templates

You can take multiple profession templates if the GM allows, but most campaigns work best with one race + one profession:

**Example Combinations:**

**Elven Wizard** = 30 CP (Elf) + 110 CP (Wizard) = 140 CP
- High DX and AW, great spellcaster
- Good with ranged weapons as backup
- Forest movement and night vision for exploration

**Dwarven Paladin** = 20 CP (Dwarf) + 75 CP (Paladin) = 95 CP
- Incredibly tough and magic resistant
- Slow but unstoppable in heavy armor
- Natural dungeon delver with divine power

**Halfling Rogue** = 20 CP (Halfling) + 38 CP (Rogue) = 58 CP
- Extremely sneaky and lucky
- Perfect scout and infiltrator
- Hard to hit, great at hiding

**Dragonborn Barbarian** = 40 CP (Dragonborn) + 40 CP (Barbarian) = 80 CP
- Massive damage output
- Natural armor + rage + breath weapon
- Terrifying in melee combat

## Creating Custom Templates

Want to make your own? Follow these guidelines:

**For Race Templates:**
1. Total should be 0-40 CP
2. Include 2-4 attribute modifiers (balanced positive and negative)
3. Include 1-3 perks that define the race
4. Add unique racial traits (no CP cost, flavor abilities)
5. Define lifespan and size

**For Profession Templates:**
1. Total should be 30-120 CP
2. Include 3-6 starting skills
3. Include 1-4 perks that define the profession
4. Add 1-3 signature abilities or starting spells/maneuvers
5. Define starting equipment package

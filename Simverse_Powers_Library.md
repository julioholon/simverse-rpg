## Building Your Own

Remember the construction system:
1. Choose base tier (Minor 1 CP / Moderate 5 CP / Major 15 CP / Epic 30 CP / Legendary 50+ CP)
2. Add enhancements (% and fixed CP)
3. Add limitations (% and fixed CP)
4. **Calculate:**
   - Apply fixed costs first: (Base + Fixed Enhancements) - Fixed Limitations
   - Then apply %: Result × (1 + Enhancement%) × (1 - Limitation%)
   - Round down, minimum 1 CP
5. Maximum -90% reduction: Final cost must be ≥ 10% of (Base + Fixed Enhancements - Fixed Limitations)

**Example Quick Build:**
Want a "Force Push" spell:
- Base: Moderate (5 CP, 2 Mana)
- Enhancement: Knockback +3 CP fixed, Multiple Targets +25%
- Limitation: Obvious -10%
- Calculation: (5 + 3) × 1.25 × 0.9 = 8 × 1.125 = 9 CP
- Effect: Push 2 targets back 2m, they roll vs ST or fall prone

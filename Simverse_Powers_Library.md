## Combat Maneuvers (Physical)

### SHIELD BASH
**Type:** Minor Physical Maneuver (1 CP)
**Resource Cost:** 1 Stamina
**Enhancements:** None
**Limitations:** Requires [Item] -10%, Melee Range Only -15%
**Calculation:** 1 × (1 + 0 - 0.10 - 0.15) = 1 × 0.75 = 0.75, round down minimum 1 CP
**Final Cost:** 1 CP

**Effect:** Make a Shield skill roll. On success, target must roll vs EN or lose their next action (stunned). Can be used as part of an attack action.

**Quick Guide to Building Summoned Creatures:**
1. **Determine CP Budget** based on spell tier (~50 CP for Moderate summons, ~75 CP for Major, ~150 CP for Epic)
2. **Choose Size Category** and apply modifiers (ST, DX, Vitality multiplier, Movement multiplier)
3. **Set Major Attributes** (10 CP per +1 from base 10, gain 10 CP per -1 for IN, WS, CH, DX)
3. **Set Minor Attributes** (5 CP per +1 from base 10, gain 5 CP per -1 for ST, EN, AW)
4. **Calculate Vitality** = EN × 2 × Size multiplier (or add More Vitality perk for +10 Vitality)
5. **Calculate Speed** = 6 × Size multiplier (base human speed is 6m per action), can use Extra Speed perk
6. **Add Natural AR** if appropriate (tough hide, scales, etc.)
7. **Add 1-3 Powers/Abilities** from remaining CP budget
8. **Keep it Simple** - summoned creatures should be easy to run in combat

## Building Your Own

Remember the construction system:
1. Choose base tier (Minor 1 CP / Moderate 5 CP / Major 15 CP / Epic 30 CP / Legendary 50+ CP)
2. Ensure you have the required skill level (Minor 2, Moderate 3, Major 5, Epic 8, Legendary 13)
3. Add percentage-based enhancements (all enhancements are now %-based)
4. Add percentage-based limitations (all limitations are now %-based)
5. **Calculate:**
   - Formula: Base × (1 + Sum of Enhancements - Sum of Limitations)
   - Round down, minimum 1 CP
6. Maximum -75% reduction: Total limitations cannot exceed -75%

**Example Quick Build:**
Want a "Force Push" spell:
- Base: Moderate (5 CP, 2 Mana, requires Spellcasting 3)
- Enhancement: Knockback +30%, Multiple Targets +50% (2 targets)
- Limitation: Obvious/Loud -10%
- Calculation: 5 × (1 + 0.30 + 0.50 - 0.10) = 5 × 1.70 = 8.5, round down to 8 CP
- Effect: Push 2 targets back 2m, they roll vs ST or fall prone

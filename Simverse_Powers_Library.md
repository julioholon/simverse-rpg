## Combat Maneuvers (Physical)

### SHIELD BASH
**Type:** Minor Physical Maneuver (5 CP)  
**Resource Cost:** 1 Stamina  
**Enhancements:** None  
**Limitations:** Requires Shield (-3 CP fixed), Requires Skill Roll -20%  
**Final Cost:** 1 CP  

**Effect:** Make a Shield skill roll. On success, target must roll vs EN or lose their next action (stunned). Can be used as part of an attack action.

---

**Enhancements:** None

**Final Cost:** 1 CP  

**Effect:** Make opposed weapon skill roll vs target. If you win by 5+ margin, target drops their weapon 2m away. Target gets +2 to resist if aware of attempt.

## Building Your Own

Remember the construction system:
1. Choose base tier (Minor/Moderate/Major/Epic)
2. Add enhancements (% and fixed)
3. Add limitations (% and fixed)
4. Calculate: Base × (1 + enhancements%) × (1 + limitations%) + fixed costs
5. Maximum -90% reduction from limitations

**Example Quick Build:**
Want a "Force Push" spell:
- Base: Moderate (10 CP, 2 Mana)
- Enhancement: Knockback +3 CP, Multiple Targets +25%
- Limitation: Obvious -10%
- Final: (10 × 1.25 × 0.9) + 3 = 14 CP
- Effect: Push 2 targets back 2m, they roll vs ST or fall prone

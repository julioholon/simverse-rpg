## Currency System

**Standard Currency: Silver Pieces (sp)**
- 1 Gold Piece (gp) = 10 Silver Pieces (sp)
- 1 Silver Piece (sp) = 10 Copper Pieces (cp)

**Starting Wealth by Background:**
- Poor/Street Urchin: 50 sp
- Common/Peasant: 100 sp
- Craftsman/Merchant: 250 sp
- Wealthy (5 CP Perk): 500 sp
- Very Wealthy (10 CP Perk): 2,500 sp
- Extremely Wealthy (15 CP Perk): 10,000 sp

## Armor

Armor is purchased by piece and protects specific body locations. **Armor Deflection (AD)** helps avoid attacks entirely, while **Armor Resistance (AR)** reduces damage from hits. Penalties apply based on the heaviest armor piece worn: **-2 for Medium, -4 for Heavy** (reduced by Armor Familiarity perks).

## Encumbrance Rules (Optional)

**Carrying Capacity:**
- **Light Load**: ST × 5 kg - No penalties
- **Medium Load**: ST × 10 kg - -2 to physical actions, -1 Speed
- **Heavy Load**: ST × 15 kg - -4 to physical actions, -2 Speed, no running
- **Maximum Drag**: ST × 30 kg - Can barely move

**Item Weights (approximate):**
- Light weapon: 1-2 kg
- Medium weapon: 2-5 kg
- Heavy weapon: 5-10 kg
- Light armor: 5-8 kg
- Medium armor: 10-15 kg
- Heavy armor: 20-30 kg
- Shield: 2-5 kg
- 100 coins: 1 kg
